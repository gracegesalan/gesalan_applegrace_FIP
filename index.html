Exploring London Canada

When people hear the word 'London' they think about London in the United Kingdom, which is understandable. But did you know there is another London in Canada? It is a city full of heritage, arts, cultural spots, and natural beauty. There are a lot of things to see and do while in the city. There is a place for you here whether you like vintage finds, art galleries, Hockey, fresh finds, and more.

London pride itself on being the center of higher education, state-of-the-art medical research, and technology in southwestern Ontario.
