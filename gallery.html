EXPLORE LONDON CANADA

DUNDAS DISCS

If you are looking for all the classic players for movies or music, this store has got your back. They sell DVDs of old and new entertainments.

ATTIC BOOKS

This is one of the best bookstore in the city. They have everything from friction to autobiography. If you are looking to spice things up on your wall, they also have artworks available.

LONDON ROCK

If you want gothic fashion and accessories this store is perfect. Find unique gems that will represent the gothic in you. They have a variety of selection that you can choose from.

CHAOTIC CLOSET

The store name may be 'chaotic' but the opposite is true when it comes to their clothes. They have a great selection of vintage clothes that is perfect for any season. They have a $10 category and it changes everyday so make sure to check them out daily.

GROOVES

Are you a music lover or just like to listen to music in the background? This store is for you! Who doesn't like music right? They have all the genre available. So they have everything for everyone.

HEROES

All things super heroes and great vibe. Every collectible imaginable can be found here. Their friendly and approachable staff makes the store a great place to browse your comics. From toys to posters they have it all.

JONATHON

Do you love ceramics? This store is the place for you. See unique and creative ceramic creations and be amazed!

LONDON MUSIC HALL OF FAME

They have a gallery of artists that made an impact in the industry. Discover local artists and learn more abou them.

DUGOUT

Statement jackets? Dugout has it! Stand out and be fashionable even during colder weathers. They have a great selection of vintage jackets that you will not find anywhere else.

TAP CENTRE FOR CREATIVITY

Check their art gallery and you may even meet and talk to the artists themselves. They showcase local great artists every other month.

LONDON MUSIC HALL

Great music and great people. That is what a memorable concert is about. This place is the place for you when you are looking for a quick break after a long week.

REBEL REMEDY

Looking for espresso drinks and healthy juices? Rebel Remedy has a great variety for you to choose from.

FILTHY REBENA VINTAGE

Luxy brands at an affordable price? Is that even possible? At Filthy Rebena Vintage, it is! Shop top fashion brands without breaking the bank.

BUDWEISER GARDENS

Watch Hockey games, concerts, stand up, and more. The Budweiser is a spot when it comes to these things. Watch your favorite artists perform along with 9 thousand other people.

COVENT MARKET

Fresh produce, spices, cheese, pastries, and all things delight. Grab a basket and fill them up with fresh local products.
