EXPLORE LONDON CANADA

It is a hub of heritage, arts, cultural spots, and natural beauty. A city filled with great art, great food, and great people. Visit London and be amazed by the city's wonder.

EXPLORE LONDON CANADA

DUNDAS DISCS

If you are looking for a nostalgia trip this place is for you. Classic game consoles and movie players. They even have a variety of classic movies available. You can travel through time and reminisce from the good old days.

LONDON ROCK

Discover unique finds and great gothic art at London Rock. This store has got everything when it comes to alternative fashion. Creative design t-shirts, jackets,earrings, caps, and more.

DUGOUT

Looking for a statement outfit? Dugout has got your back! They have a variety of vintage jackets, sweaters, and shirts waiting for you to rock them. You will never find another clothing items similar to what you will find here.

TAP

Treat your eyes with amazing art by visiting TAP. It is a center for creativity in Downtown London. They showcase local arts from local artists. And if you get lukcy, you may even get to meet and talk to the artist behind the art.

HEROES

All things super heroes and great vibe. Every collectible imaginable can be found here. Their friendly and approachable staff makes the store a great place to browse your comics.

GROOVES

Are you a music lover or just like to listen to music in the background? This store is for you! Who doesn't like music right? They have all the genre available. So they have everything for everyone.
